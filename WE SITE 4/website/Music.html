Music I Like

Rock Music Pop Music Rap Music

ROCK MUSIC

Beatles, Fleetwood Mac, Pink Floyd and The Pixies populate this list.

The Beatles- While My Guitar Gently Weeps

My favorite Beatles song.
Written and performed by George Harrison,
featuring guitar by Eric Clapton.

Fleetwood Mac- The Chain

My favorite FleetWood Mac song.
From their famous album Rumours,
the album about breakups and divorce.
This song is the only one that all 5 contributed to writing

The Pixies- Where Is My Mind

My favorite Pixies song.
I heard this song live in Galway
It was fantastic.
Very unique and creative writing.

Pink Floyd- Time

My favorite Pink Floyd song
One of their longer songs, featuring heavy instrumental sections
From Dark Side of the Moon

POP MUSIC

Tame Impala, Gorillaz, and ABBA populate this list.

Tame Impala- Eventually

My favorite Tame Impala song.
Has a strong guitar tone and bassline

Gorillaz- Opium

My favorite Gorillaz song.
Features catchy verses and great instrumentals
as well as a great feature from EARTHGANG

ABBA- Lay All Your Love On Me

My favorite ABBA song.
Just a class song

RAP MUSIC

Ye, Tyler The Creator and Kendrick populate this list.

Kanye West- Moon

My favorite Kanye song
From his studio album Donda, named after his late mother.

Tyler, The Creator- New Magic Wand

My favorite Tyler song.
From his famous concept album IGOR
A strong, heavy, dark song.

Kendrick Lamar- Family Ties

My favorite Kendrick song.
Written and performed with his cousin, Baby Keem.
